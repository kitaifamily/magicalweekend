Stay

Hotels

After scouring London for somewhere special, we have two hotels to recommend. They each offer a very different London experience, but both are wonderfully located and equally convenient for the wedding weekend.

Durrants Hotel

For something sweet and traditional, we would love to suggest Durrants Hotel in Marylebone.

One of the last family-owned hotels in the area, you will be warmly greeted and surrounded in a quintessentially British design. We have arranged rates for our guests at £235 a night excluding breakfast.

Please feel free to contact Neesa at Salesoffice@DurrantsHotel.co.uk to secure a room.

Visit Durrants Hotel

St Clement’s Hotel

For something that celebrates London’s contemporary design scene, we would love to recommend St Clement’s Hotel on the Strand.

One of London’s newest hotels, St Clement’s has been described as the child of Claridge’s and Soho House—making it feel like a rather fitting choice for our wedding weekend.
